Privacy Policy – Family Room App

Effective Date: 17 Feb 2025
Last Updated: 29 Dec 2025

Welcome to Family Room App. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile details (if provided)
  • User ID
  • Room / group related information

Location Data:

  • Foreground location
  • Background location (when enabled)

Location data is used to:

  • Show real-time location of room members
  • Enable live map view while chatting
  • Display movement history for up to 3 days
Location data is shared only with members of the same room and can be enabled or disabled at any time from device settings or within the app.

Device & Usage Information:

  • Device model
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • IP address
  • App usage logs
  • Crash reports
  • Last login timestamp

2. Payment / UPI Support

IMPORTANT:

  • We do NOT collect payment data
  • We do NOT store UPI or bank details
  • We do NOT store transaction references

7. Children’s Privacy (Under 13 Years)

Family Room is designed for family use and may include children under 13 years of age.

  • Accounts must be created and managed by a parent or legal guardian
  • No personal data is knowingly collected without parental consent
  • If such data is identified, it will be deleted immediately
